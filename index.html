About This Platform

The Serrano Lab Mentoring & Training Platform supports structured, accountable mentoring in academic research. Mentee and Mentor portals provide complementary tools for IDP tracking, professional development, milestone scheduling, and generating documentation for fellowships, grants, and reviews.
